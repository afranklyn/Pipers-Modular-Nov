Where to find us

We're open every day of the year except Christmas and Boxing Day.

During summer we are open from 7:30am until late, serving breakfast, lunch, dinner and takeaway.

In the winter months we are open for breakfast and lunch every day, and for dinner on Friday and Saturday nights.
